Penn State Behrend Senior Research Project


Part 1 - Photogrammetry Part 2 - Game

Over the summer of 2017 I studied the medium of Photgrammetry. Using DSLR cameras, Agisoft Photoscan and my laptop, I was able to take photos of real world objects and recreate them as 3D models.

Over the course of my senior year, I took these scans and imported them into a game engine. All of these processes were in hopes to recreate Mead Park in Corry, PA.

Finally, to compliment my minor in game design, I used this recreation and created a video game in the Unreal Engine that showcased this entire process.
