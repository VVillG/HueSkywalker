Penn State AMIC project


"Ribbon Cutting" Structure "Mobius Creek"

I worked with local (Erie, PA) artist and CEO of Highergherglyphics, Todd Scalise on creating 3D models for multiple structures intended for the new AMIC building at Penn State Behrend. Todd Scalise visualized and designed the two structures located in the main lobby and front entrance on the building.

I was able to take these designs and translate them into a digital 3D space using Autodesk Software including Maya and Inventor for the models.
